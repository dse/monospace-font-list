## The Couriers

There are sixty five krillion implementations of IBM's public
domain(?) Courier typeface.  This may or may not be a complete
list of them.

-   IBM Courier
-   Courier New
    -   Courier New is one of Microsoft's Core Fonts for the Web.
-   Courier Prime
    -   Courier Prime Code
    -   Courier Prime Sans
-   Courier Screenplay
-   Courier Final Draft
-   Dark Courier
-   Courier 10 Pitch BT
    -   foundry: Bitstream
-   Courier Code
    -   based on: Courier 10 Pitch BT
-   Courier Standard
    -   distributed with Adobe Reader 6
-   Nimbus Mono L
    -   foundry: URW++
    -   one of the Ghostscript fonts
-   TeX Gyre Cursor
    -   based on: Nimbus Mono L
-   FreeMono
    -   based on: Nimbus Mono L
-   EF Courier B
-   Courier Line Draw
-   Courier M
-   Courier MM Screenwriter
    -   bundled with Movie Magic Screenwriter 6
